About This Project

This initiative is part of The Mythic Society Bengaluru Inscriptions 3D Digital Conservation Project. It aims to digitally preserve and showcase Bengaluru's rich heritage, including ancient temples, herostones, and inscriptions, through interactive maps and 3D models.

  • Explore interactive maps of heritage sites
  • View 3D digital reconstructions
  • Browse photos and historical details
  • Learn about the conservation efforts
